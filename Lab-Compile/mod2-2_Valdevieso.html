Oshi No Ko

Oshi No Ko is about two siblings who set off to the entertainment world after the tragic murder of a famous, world-renowned idol, and single mother Ai Hoshino. Ruby Hoshino joined the entertainment world to become a famous and world-renowned idol just like her mother. Aqua Hoshino sets off to the entertainment world for the sole purpose to find the one who ordered to murder his mother which he suspect to be his biological father.

Top 5 favorite characters in Oshi No Ko

  1. Ai Hoshino
  2. Kana Arima
  3. Akane Kurokowa
  4. Ruby Hoshino
  5. Miyako Saitou
